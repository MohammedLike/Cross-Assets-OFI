Backtesting Methodology

Execution Model

  • Walk-forward: 6-month train, 1-month OOS
  • Position sizing: signal-proportional, normalised to [-1, +1]
  • Lagged positions (no look-ahead bias)
  • Position updated every bar

Cost Scenarios

  • Baseline: 0 bps (theoretical maximum)
  • Low Cost: 3 bps (institutional / HFT)
  • Realistic: 7 bps (prop trading desk)
  • High Cost: 15 bps (retail execution)

Key Takeaway: The gap between the "No Costs" and "Realistic" PnL curves quantifies the implementable alpha. If the realistic-cost curve remains upward-sloping, the signal has genuine economic value. If it flattens or turns negative, the signal is there but not tradable at this frequency.
