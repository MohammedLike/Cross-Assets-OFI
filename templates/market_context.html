Why fuse news with OFI?

OFI tells us what the market is doing. News tells us why. The RAG pipeline retrieves recent financial headlines from Indian sources (Moneycontrol, Economic Times, Mint, Business Standard), embeds them with sentence-transformers, indexes them in FAISS, and converts each bar into a small set of news features: article count in window, average sentiment, asset-specific mention counts. These augment the Ridge feature matrix to test whether information shocks add predictive power beyond microstructure alone.
